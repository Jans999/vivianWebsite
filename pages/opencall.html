Open call for Greener Grass community project

Vivian Triantafyllopoulou is looking for people to join her community project ‘Greener Grass’ which will be performed on the 16th May at John Lyon’s theatre, London as an opening for the premiere of the piece ‘Ego Mas’.

‘Greener Grass’ is based on the idea that we think the grass is always greener on the other side and the sky more blue somewhere else… Bringing ideas from culture, home, Europe, friendship, travellig, we will create together a 10’-15’ piece which will open the performance evening. No professional dancing experience is necessary, just passion for moving, willingness to perform on stage, working with a team and feeling comfortable with contact work and the use of voice!

Participants must be available for all the following dates for rehearsal:

  • Sunday 5th April: 12:00-14:00 at Chisenhale Dance
  • Sunday 26th April: 12:00-14:00 at Chisenhale Dance
  • Sunday 3rd May: 10:00-13:00 at Trinity Laban
  • Sunday 10th May: 10:00-13:00 at Trinity Laban
  • Saturday 16th May: From 15:00, show at 20:00, John Lyons Theatre

Due to limited spaces the participants will be chosen on a first come first served basis. Participation is free of charge. Refreshments will be provided for rehearsals. If you are interested please send an email to:

info@viviantr.com

Thank you! Looking forward to hear from you!
