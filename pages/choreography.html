Ego Mas

An international collaboration between Vivian Triantafyllopoulou's dance company based in London with the Athens based band Sevengill

A choreography that explores human relationships by observing where and how we find motivation and the power to move fowards - alone or with others. What makes us want to wake up in the morning and how can we influence each other?

A unique meeting of talented dancers from London and a Greek post metal band that sparks a dance collaboration with a genre of music that we are not used to.
